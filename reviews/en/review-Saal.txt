But, unexpectedly, it was not a trap ! So, I really enjoyed to use it and create my own photo book about my holidays in Alps.</p>

<h2>The creation with Saal's tool</h2>

<p>The creation is done thanks to their software available for more or less all platforms or OS, except Linux. In my case, one of my computers is under Windows, so I could download and use their application. 

<br>To download their tool, you just need to go one their <a href="https://www.saal-digital.com/professional-line/" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">website</span></a>. It is really easy.</p>

<p>Their tool is also really easy to use and very intuitive. They propose quick tutorials to handle it ! It took me only few minutes to understand it, and start my book. It is also in this application that you can chose the size, cover, number of pages, etc. of your book.</p> 

<div class="photo-box pure-u-1 pure-u-md-1-2 pure-u-lg-1-3">
<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/OJSMBENInpo">
</iframe>
</div>

<p>For my part, I preferred to add some money in order to:
<li>Have a special cover with black leather and a glass plate to see a picture first,
<li>Have a big format (30x40cm),</li>
<li>Have more pages,</li>
<li>Have a mate paper.</li><br>

However, with the voucher given, I could just create a simpler book with leather cover, no glass, less pages, without paying anything, which is after all, a good solution too. It was a real opportinity to test their product for nothing than a review. Really a good deal for me ! </p>

<p>Yes, I can understand that without the voucher, it can be expensive to buy but.. I challenge any other company to propose such a simple tool to create a book for a less cost ! And, as we are going to see, with the same quality in the final product. <br>
For that point, despite the pice, I really give a good point to Saal.</p>

<h2>The photo book : final product from Saal</h2>

<p> I got to wait a bit before receiving my book, but it was a real pleasure when it arrived ! <br><br>

I was concerned for the quality, colour and paper.. After all, the printing can be really different from what you see on your computer or camera and thus, from what you expect, if one of them is not correctly calibrated.<br>
But honnestly, the final product is perfect for me ! The quality is here as are the colours. </p>


<p>Before letting you see what the book looks like, I will conclude: 
<ul>
<li>Positive:
    <ul>
        <li>The printing quality is perfect. The visual appearance of their tool for creation corresponds exactly to the final product on paper. No bad surprise here,</li>
        <li>The creation tool is really easy to handle, intuitive, downloadable free,</li>
        <li>They are many creation options to chose, and many tutorials to see,</li>
        <li>The delivery has been quick. The book was correctly protected for the travel which is something I really appreciate. It makes me think that they take care of our possessions.</li>
    </ul></li>
<li>Negative:
    <ul>
        <li>The price is a bit expensive when you want to make a boot for yourself, but all the previous cited qualities balance completely this aspect,</li>
        <li>The tool is not compatible with Linux, and I exclusively work on Linux, so I consider it as a bad point.</li>
    </ul></li>
</ul></p>

<p>Finally, I find more positive than negative points to Saal, and I think I will probably command a new book soon. This voucher helps me to test their products, and to make a proper opinion of them. Currently, I am really satisfied of their services ! </p>
